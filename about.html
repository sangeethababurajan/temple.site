#Know about us

WHO WE ARE?

Service Provider of agarbatti stick, fragranced incense sticks & other products in Kanchipuram, Tamil Nadu.A small scale business organisation which tends to provide quality products to customers with love and care. By helping middle class families to work in the organisation and grow together. We help people achieve independently by making it easier to start, run and grow a business. We provide them with the initial investment as the products itself.

History of Kaasi Poojas

In 2015...

The idea started with around 3 people working with 4 machines, which then developed day by day by providing services from all over state crossed different states of India. Bangalore was the first city to give orders, gradually this increased.

In 2018...

The company grew by having lot of workers and started providing the products all over world. Main mission is to make our product reach nook and corner of the world by spreading positivity and spirituality through the fragrance.
