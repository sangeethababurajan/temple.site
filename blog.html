Rudraksh-its significance

A rudraksha is the seed of the Elaeocarpus ganitrus tree and plays an important role in a spiritual seeker's life. Wearing Rudraksha brings the grace of Lord Shiva upon you. It surrounds you with positive vibrations. Rudraksha will shield you from negative forces. It blesses a devotee with knowledge, power.It is for general wellbeing, health and freedom. It lowers your blood pressure, calms your nerves and brings a certain calmness and alertness in your nervous system.

Sambrani-organic clean fragrance

A Sambrani Cup is a natural incense vessel crafted from a combination of aromatic resins and herbs. These cups, often small in size, hold a blend of fragrant materials that, when ignited, release a soothing, mystical smoke. Sambrani cups are also known as Benzoin cups. The cups are filled with divine aroma of ayurvedic herbs like Loban, Jatamasi, Guggal, Kapoor kachri, Ghee & other healing herbs. They are free from toxins and charcoal smoke.

Ebony tree wood(Karungali)

Ebony wood, commonly referred to as Karungali, is an energy booster known for promoting health, wealth, and prosperity as well as alleviating depression and anxiety.Karungali-infused water can aid in healing rashes, wounds, scars, acne and psoriasis as well as balancing the tridooshas of pittha, kapha and vattha in your body. Furthermore, it strengthens mental clarity by relieving depression while stimulating positive thinking.

Panchagavya-deepam

Panchgavya represents milk, urine, dung, ghee, and curd, derived from cow and serves irreplaceable medicinal importance in Ayurveda and traditional Indian clinical practices. Panchgavya can also give back to mother nature by promoting soil fertility, earthworm production, protecting crops from bacterial and fungal infections, etc. It is equally important to educate the people and promote Panchgavya products to seek the world's attention towards India's rich traditional practice and literature.
